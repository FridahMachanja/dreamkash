PRIVACY POLICY

Overview

Poollend is an Intermediary Financial Service Provider which seeks to connect its’ clientele with fit and proper financial assistance.

In terms of the Protection of Private Information Act No.4 of 2013 (hereafter “POPI”), and in line with our company policy, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your private information will be used in accordance with the relevant and applicable law.

Section 9 of POPI states that “Personal Information may only be processed if, given the purpose for which it is processed, it is adequate, relevant and not excessive.”

Collection of Personal Information

Poollend collects and processes its clients’ personal information, pertaining to the clients’ financial needs. The type of information will depend on the need for which it is collected, the information will be processed and will be for that purpose and other uses the company needs the information for in order to fulfil the financial needs of the client. Whenever possible, Poollend will inform the client when there is information required, as the information may be used for research purposes by Poollend as well.

Safeguarding of Client Personal Information

It is a requirement of the Protection of Personal Information Act to adequately protect personal information. We as Poollend will continuously review our security controls and processes to ensure that personal information is secure.

As the transfer of information across border lines is not permitted without the consent of whom the information belongs to, whether the transfer of information is for purpose of storage or usage, we as Poollend will ensure that the information they receive (that being the 3rd party from us will be treated with the same level of assured security as we treat the information we are entrusted with.

Storage of Personal Information

Poollend’s customer data is stored in databases that have built-in safeguards and firewalls to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of that information. We protect that information using appropriate encryption technology. As the Website is automated, none of our employees will have access to your Personal Information. We store data in encrypted form on computers and control access via secure web pages. We employ firewalls and other security technologies to protect our servers from external attack.
