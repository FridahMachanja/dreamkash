Why Dreamkash?

We empower companies to provide credit facilities and salary advances to employees

Quick business loans can also be offered to members of a closed group such as credit unions and cooperative savings

Our systems are fully automated and work 24hrs a day, allowing emergency loan requests at any time of the day or night.

Here is how it works

  • For companies

    Company will upload the list of employees or members and set a credit limit for each of them

    Dreamkash loads the sufficient funds that can be lent out to the employees or members

    A link to download the Dreamkash app is sent to all employees and report summaries will be shared

  • For employees or members

    Receive link to download the Dreamkash app from your company and register to create your account

    Once verified, a dashboard will pop up displaying your credit limit and different credit options.

    On request for a loan, the money is sent to the mobile money account number that you registered
